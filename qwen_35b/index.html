About Paris

A timeless romance in the heart of France

Paris, the capital and most populous city of France, has been one of Europe's major centres of finance, commerce, culture, arts, fashion, and science since the 17th century. Known as the "City of Light", Paris offers an unforgettable experience with its iconic landmarks, world-class museums, exquisite cuisine, and romantic ambiance.

From the majestic Eiffel Tower to the serene Seine River, every corner of Paris tells a story. Whether you're exploring historic neighborhoods, indulging in gourmet dining, or discovering hidden gems, Paris will captivate your heart and soul.

Top Attractions

Must-visit landmarks and experiences

Eiffel Tower

The iconic iron lattice tower and symbol of Paris, offering breathtaking views from its observation decks.

⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

Louvre Museum

The world's largest art museum, home to thousands of works including the Mona Lisa and Venus de Milo.

⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

Notre-Dame

A masterpiece of French Gothic architecture, renowned for its stunning stained glass and historic significance.

⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

Arc de Triomphe

An iconic monument honoring those who fought for France, with panoramic views of the Champs-Élysées.
